Our Mission

Dedicated to Excellence Excellence is the gradual result of always striving to do better. Sadguru's Mission

Maxfort School Dwarka's mission is to provide education and to encourage 'Lateral', 'Creative' and 'Innovative' thinking which would propel students towards a world of continuous change and empower them to manage their future.


Intelligent questions. Perplexing questions. Confusing questions,Probing questions.


Questions keep the world ticking. At Maxfort we understand that the curious mind of a child is a happy cauldron of questions. And therefore we ensure that every time a student raises her or his hand to ask a question, there should be no fear in mind. Every question is answered patiently.


To bring out the real potential of a child it is imperative to give wings to imagination. What? Why? How? When? Where? At Maxfort, learning goes beyond the words imprinted in the book. In fact it's just a part of holistic learning. Here teachers are mentors. And the world is the exploration ground.
